Pozole is one of Mexico's most delicious dishes and even though it is labor intensive, it is a very special dish. This dish can be served for special events of all kinds and can be adjusted to the liking of each individual. In fact, there are hundreds of combinations of pozole you can try and with different spices or meats! Remember, one type of pozole is not enough!

Ingredients

  1. 2 lbs pork shoulder (or pork loin)
  2. 1 large white onion, halved
  3. 4–5 garlic cloves
  4. 2 bay leaves
  5. 1 tbsp salt (adjust to taste)
  6. Water (enough to cover pork, ~10 cups)
  7. 1–2 cans (25 oz each) hominy, drained and rinsed
  8. 5 dried guajillo chiles
  9. 3 dried ancho chiles
  10. 2 dried chiles de árbol (optional for spice)
  11. 3 garlic cloves
  12. 1/4 onion
  13. 1 tsp oregano
  14. 1/2 tsp cumin
  15. Salt to taste
  16. 1–2 cups of pork broth (from the pot)

Time to cook!

In a large pot, add:

  • 2 lbs pork shoulder
  • 1 halved white onion
  • 4–5 garlic cloves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • About 10 cups of water (enough to fully cover the pork)

    • Next, the sauce:

    Remove the stems and seeds from the chiles. In a small pot, add the dried chiles and cover them with water. Bring the water to a boil, then turn off the heat and let the chiles soak for about 15–20 minutes until they are softened. Once softened, place the chiles in a blender along with 3 garlic cloves, 1/4 onion, 1 teaspoon of oregano, 1/2 teaspoon of cumin, and 1–2 cups of pork broth. Blend everything until the mixture becomes completely smooth. Finally, strain the sauce through a mesh strainer into a bowl to remove any remaining skins or seeds.

    bringing it all together

    Add the shredded pork back into the large pot with the reserved broth. Pour in the red chile sauce. Add: 1–2 cans hominy (drained and rinsed), Bring everything to a gentle boil, Reduce heat and simmer for 20–30 minutes, allowing flavors to blend. Taste and adjust salt if needed.

    For serving, add:
    • Shredded cabbage or lettuce
    • Diced onion
    • Sliced radishes
    • Lime wedges
    • Oregano
    • Crushed tortilla chips or tostadas
